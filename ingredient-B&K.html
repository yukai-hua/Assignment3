ingredients

Spice:

2 teaspoons ground cumin 2 teaspoons ground coriander 1 teaspoon ground paprika 1 teaspoon ground sumac (optional) 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon 1/4 teaspoon ground allspice

Seasoned Flour:

2 cups all-purpose flour 3 teaspoons kosher salt 1 tablespoon freshly ground black pepper

Savory Beef:

3 pounds ground beef (80% lean) 1 large onion, finely grated 3 cloves garlic, minced 1/2 cup fresh parsley, finely chopped Salt, to taste Olive oil, for brushing

Instructions:

Grill kebabs for about 4-5 minutes on each side, or until fully cooked. Serve hot with pita bread, salad, and your favorite sauces.
