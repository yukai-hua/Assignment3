ingredients

Fresh Greens:

4 cups fresh spinach leaves 4 cups Romaine lettuce, chopped 1 large avocado, sliced 1 cup sweet corn kernels

Dressing:

2 tablespoons lemon juice 1/4 cup olive oil Salt and pepper, to taste

Cheese and Herbs:

Sprinkle the crumbled feta cheese over the salad. Add the chopped chives and parsley.

Instructions:

Grill kebabs for about 4-5 minutes on each side, or until fully cooked. Serve hot with pita bread, salad, and your favorite sauces.
